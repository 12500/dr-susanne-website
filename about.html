About Me

I am a resourceful and productive researcher who takes pride and initiative in going above set objectives. I am a pleasant and keen learner who finds work within collaborative partnerships exciting. I lead the NETS research group which undertakes research in Networked Systems and Systems Security. Our flagship research project is  WIMEA-ICT  in which we have developed low cost automatic weather stations, weather forecast modeling and software development for weather services. I lead the Excellent Ladies Ministry and my ministry passion is that each woman courageously  press on to their best self.
