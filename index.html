Julianne Susanne Otim (P.h.D)

I am a Senior Lecturer in Computer Networks at Makerere University where I teach, undertake research and consultancy related to applying Information and Communication Technologies  (ICTs) to different development contexts. I have published widely with over 30 research articles and have supervised over 20 graduate students. I also serve on several boards including that of Research and Education Network for Uganda (RENU) and the UbuntuNet Alliance

I am married to Pastor Arthur Otim, with whom we pastor Miracle Center Seeta Church and we are blessed with three amazing sons: Baraka, Ajabu and Amani as well as many spiritual children.
